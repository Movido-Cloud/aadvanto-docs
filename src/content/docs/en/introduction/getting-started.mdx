Welcome to the Advanto Billing System! Our platform is designed to streamline your billing and customer management processes, making it easier for you to manage your business operations efficiently. This guideline will walk you through the various features and functionalities of the Advanto Billing System, providing you with the necessary knowledge to utilize the system to its full potential.

### Understanding the System

The Advanto Billing System offers a comprehensive suite of tools to manage customers, transactions, invoices, and more. Below are the key components you'll find within the system:

1. **Customers**: Manage your customer database, add new customers, and view or edit existing customer details.
2. **Bank**: Handle SEPA Direct Debits, manage bank accounts, and process transactions.
3. **Accounting**: Keep track of financial transactions, export invoices, and manage bank accounts.
4. **Tickets**: Manage customer support tickets and inquiries efficiently.
5. **Reports**: Access detailed reports and analytics to monitor business performance.
6. **Templates**: Configure and customize email and billing templates to suit your business needs.
7. **Delivery Logs**: Track the status of emails sent to customers.
8. **Administration**: Manage user roles, system logs, products, tax rules, subcontractors, and tenants.

### Getting Started

To begin using the Advanto Billing System, follow these steps:

- **User-Friendly Interface**: The system is designed with an intuitive interface, making it easy for users to navigate and find the information they need.
- **Comprehensive Customer Management**: Add, view, and edit customer details seamlessly.
- **Efficient Transaction Handling**: Manage SEPA Direct Debits and other financial transactions with ease.
- **Detailed Reporting**: Generate and view detailed reports to keep track of business performance.
- **Customizable Templates**: Create and manage templates for emails and invoices to maintain consistent communication with customers.
