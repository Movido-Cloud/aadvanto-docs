Logging into the Advanto Billing System is the first step to accessing its powerful features. This section will guide you through the login process, ensuring you can quickly and easily get started with managing your billing and customer operations.

### Steps to Log In

#### Step 1: Open the Login Page

1. **URL**: Open your web browser and navigate to the Advanto Billing System URL provided by your administrator.
2. **Browser Compatibility**: Ensure you are using a supported browser for the best experience (e.g., Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge).

#### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

1. **Username**: In the username field, enter your assigned username. This is typically your email address or a unique identifier provided by your administrator.
2. **Password**: In the password field, enter your secure password. Ensure that your password is kept confidential to protect your account.

#### Step 3: Click on the Login Button

1. **Login Button**: After entering your credentials, click the "Login" button to proceed.
2. **Remember Me**: Optionally, you can check the "Remember Me" box to stay logged in on your device. Use this feature only on personal or secure devices.

#### Troubleshooting Login Issues

- **Forgot Password**: If you have forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
- **Account Locked**: If your account is locked due to multiple unsuccessful login attempts, contact your administrator to unlock your account.
- **Browser Issues**: Ensure that your browser is up-to-date and that cookies and JavaScript are enabled.

### Dashboard Overview

After successfully logging in, you will be directed to the dashboard. The dashboard provides a quick overview of key metrics and navigational elements to help you get started:

1. **Total Customers**: View the total number of customers in the system.
2. **Customers Added Today**: See how many new customers were added today.
3. **Customers Added in the Last 30 Days**: Monitor the number of new customers added in the past month.

Logging into the Advanto Billing System is a straightforward process designed to secure your data while providing quick access to all features. If you encounter any issues during the login process, refer to the troubleshooting section or contact your administrator for assistance.
